ABOUT US

Board Game Café is a coffee house where the main attraction are board games!

Found in 2021, it has since become a popular attraction in Lisbon.

The decoration pulls you to a fantasy world where your imagination and achievements are king. Everything has thematic names, from the tables to the food and drinks that go along with the more than 300 different games we have.

Register, choose a title, make a reservation and soon you’ll unlock the right to be called John, Lord of the Cosmos or Queen of Myths Jane.

We’ll be eagerly awaiting for the day you come roll the dice!
